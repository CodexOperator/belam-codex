# Research-First Mandate — Prove Signal Before Profit

## Decision
The project goal is traceable signal extraction — proving or disproving that systematic patterns exist in microcap meme token price action — before optimizing for profit.

## Implications
- Start at $5–10 per trade (invisible to all markets including thin microcap pools)
- Half-Kelly sizing only AFTER signal confidence and win-rate are established
- 2+ weeks paper trading before any real capital
- Anti-overfitting protocol is non-negotiable: walk-forward, purged CV, deflated Sharpe, multiple hypothesis correction
- Every experiment logged with full template — no undocumented runs
- BTC control comparison required — meme signals must beat BTC to justify additional risk
