### Key findings
1. **30m sweet spot** for all tokens, 15m also strong for SOL specifically
2. **4h regime TF** wins for regime gating (matches model's fractal feature hierarchy: models use higher-TF RSI)
3. **highvol_only** regime gating is best DD reducer; opposite-direction blocking actually INCREASES DD
4. **chop_only** gating preserves best Sharpe
5. **hybrid_k2n3 sizing** (linear steps dampened by sigmoid) is the practical winner: 122x growth at 5x leverage with 26% DD
6. **linear_progressive** gets insane growth (17,000x+) but with 5000%+ DD — needs regime gating to tame
7. **Leverage scales linearly** in Calmar ratio for SOL 15m hybrid: constant ~12.3 growth/DD ratio across 5x-14x
8. **Tighter SL (0.5x ATR) hurts** at high leverage — getting stopped on noise then missing recovery
9. **V2 Sharpe was broken** (97+, per-trade annualized); V3 fixed to equity-curve daily Sharpe (realistic 8-14 range)
10. **Cross-asset flag** (var a vs b) had negligible impact on model performance
11. **0.80 confidence** is the sweet spot (V4 finding, missed in V5's grid, restored in V6)
