### Research Packet / Knowledge Promotion System ‚Äî Design Finalized
Shael brought a framework from @meta_alchemist (via Grok) about progressive knowledge distillation. Adapted it to our existing primitive system:

**Core design decisions:**
- Lessons and decisions ARE the research packets ‚Äî no new `research-packets/` directory
- Knowledge wiki pages (`knowledge/`) ARE the meta-doctrines (higher-level synthesis)
- Only 2 new YAML fields: `promotion_status` (exploratory‚Üícandidate‚Üípromoted‚Üívalidated) and `doctrine_richness` (0-10, auto-computed)
- Dropped `doctrine_tags` ‚Äî existing `tags` field already serves this purpose (filter out `instance:*` when grouping)
- Claim/Mechanism/Boundary/Contradiction body format applies to knowledge wiki synthesis only, NOT to individual lessons/decisions
- Quality gate checks existing lesson/decision section formats (Context/What Happened/Lesson/Application for lessons; Context/Options/Decision/Consequences for decisions)
- Promotion is one-directional ‚Äî no demotion, use `superseded_by` for retirement
- `contradicts: []` field added for bidirectional contradiction edges (different from superseded ‚Äî both valid simultaneously, interference patterns worth studying)
- Memory entries bypass promotion gate in weekly sync (no promotion_status field)
- `weekly_knowledge_sync.py` exists but was never added to cron ‚Äî adding at Monday 03:30 UTC after weekly consolidation (03:00)

### Key architectural insight
The Claim/Mechanism/Boundary/Contradiction format maps to a HIGHER level of abstraction than individual lessons. Lessons record "what happened" ‚Äî knowledge wiki entries synthesize "what's universally true and when it breaks." The promotion ladder bridges these levels.
